About the Centre

Rajesh Kumar — Founder & Lead Teacher

I did my B.Sc. from CCS University, Meerut and a B.Ed. from MDU, Rohtak. I taught Class 9–12 Math at a government school in Bulandshahr from 2008 to 2012. I left because I wanted to give more attention to students than the 1:60 ratio allowed. I opened Excel Coaching in Vaishali in January 2010 with 8 students in one room.

Today we have 4 teachers, approximately 100 active students, and a waiting list for some batches. That sounds like bragging but I'm just stating facts — the reason we have a waiting list is that I refuse to add students beyond what our batch capacity allows, even when parents request it.

My Teaching Philosophy

"I don't think every child needs to become a topper. I think every child deserves to understand what's being taught to them. If your child finishes Class 10 with a genuine understanding of basic algebra and science — not just the ability to pass — they're ahead of 80% of students entering Class 11."
